Cesium 3D Unemployment Map

HTML, CSS, Js, GeoJSON

An interactive CesiumJS globe visualizing U.S. county‐level unemployment: CSV data is converted to JSON, merged with GeoJSON boundaries, and rendered as a dynamic choropleth. Counties shade by unemployment rate, states extrude on selection, and users toggle color‐blind–safe palettes and layers—demonstrating real-world data integration in WebGL.
